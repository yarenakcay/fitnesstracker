Cardio

Cardiovascular exercise, commonly known as cardio, is any type of exercise that raises your heart rate and helps strengthen your heart and lungs. It plays a vital role in maintaining a healthy body weight, improving circulation, and boosting endurance.

Key Benefits of Cardio:

  • Burns calories and promotes fat loss
  • Enhances heart and lung health
  • Reduces the risk of chronic diseases such as hypertension and diabetes
  • Improves mood and reduces anxiety through endorphin release
  • Increases stamina and energy levels

Popular Cardio Exercises:

  • Running & Jogging: Great for building endurance and burning fat.
  • Cycling: Low-impact yet effective for strengthening lower-body muscles.
  • Swimming: Full-body workout that is easy on the joints.
  • Jump Rope: Compact and efficient for burning high calories in a short time.
  • HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training): Combines short bursts of intense activity with recovery periods for maximum efficiency.
